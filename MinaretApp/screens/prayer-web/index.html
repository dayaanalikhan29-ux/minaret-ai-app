Salah Guide

📖 Complete Salah Guide (2 Rakat)

🕋

Niyyah (Intention)

No need to say it out loud. Make intention in your heart for the specific Salah (e.g., "I am praying 2 rakats Fajr for the sake of Allah").

👐

Takbir al-Ihram

Raise both hands up to ears (men) / shoulders (women). Say: "Allahu Akbar".

الله أكبر
🤲

Opening Supplication (Optional Sunnah)

"Subhanak-Allahumma wa bihamdika wa tabaarakasmuka wa ta'ala jadduka wa la ilaaha ghayruk."

📖

Recite Surah Al-Fatiha

"Bismillah-ir-Rahman-ir-Raheem..." Then say: Ameen.

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
📖

Recite Another Surah

Example: Surah Ikhlas, Surah Kausar, etc.

🙇

Rukoo (Bowing)

Bow with back straight. Say: "Subhana Rabbiyal Azeem" (3x or more).

سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الْعَظِيمِ
🧍‍♂️

Stand Up from Rukoo

Say: "Sami Allahu liman Hamidah". Then: "Rabbana lakal hamd".

🤲

Sujood (Prostration)

Go into prostration. Say: "Subhana Rabbiyal A'la" (3x or more).

سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الْأَعْلَى
🪑

Sit Between Sujood

Sit and say: "Rabbighfir li, Rabbighfir li".

🙇‍♂️

Second Sujood

Repeat prostration with same tasbih.

🔁

Now Stand for Second Rakat

Repeat steps: Surah Fatiha, Another Surah, Rukoo, Sujood (2x).

🪑

Final Tashahhud (Sitting After Second Rakat)

Recite: "At-tahiyyatu lillahi..." "Allahumma salli 'ala Muhammad..."

🫱

Tasleem (End of Salah)

Turn your head right: "Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullah". Then left: "Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullah".

✅

Salah Complete

Your 2-rakat Salah is now complete.

📌 Important Notes

ℹ️

If it's a 3 or 4 rakat prayer (e.g. Dhuhr), you stand up after the second rakah and complete the rest silently.

ℹ️

In final sitting (last rakah), you recite full Tashahhud + Salawat + Dua, then tasleem.

ℹ️

This format is for obligatory Salah, not for Eid or Janazah — those have different rules.
